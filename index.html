FinTech · PINNs
01
Hybrid Black-Scholes Option Pricing via PINNs
Physics-informed Neural Networks · PyTorch
Problem
Classical Black-Scholes solvers ignore boundary condition violations common in real market data — mispricing risk in stressed regimes.
Approach
Physics-informed neural network embedding the Black-Scholes PDE directly as a loss term, forcing the model to respect the governing equation while fitting market observations.
Outcome
Superior boundary adherence vs vanilla neural net baselines. Generalises across strike/maturity grids with physically consistent Greeks.
Why it matters
Bridges a decade of IFRS financial modelling with scientific ML — a rare cross-disciplinary capability in quantitative finance.
